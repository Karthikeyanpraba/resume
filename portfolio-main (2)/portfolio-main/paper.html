Description:

Voting is a crucial right where citizens pick their leaders, but current methods can be insecure. E-voting with blockchain offers a solution. It makes voting safe, transparent, and fraud-resistant. Each vote is a block, maintaining accuracy and honesty. The blockchain, secure and transparent, records votes on a peer-to-peer network. It's unchangeable, encrypting data and using smart contracts to prevent fake votes. This research explains how blockchain records votes, emphasizing its structure and benefits for electronic voting.
